Sumedang Moving Towards Smart City Through TV Robots

FAJARNUSANTARA.COM- The Sumedang Regency Government has once again made a digital breakthrough by launching TV Robot, an interactive media platform designed to expand the reach of public information while strengthening community participation.

The Sumedang Regency Government has taken another progressive step in accelerating the development of a digital government ecosystem. On Tuesday, June 24, 2025, Sumedang Regent H. Dony Ahmad Munir officially launched TV Robot at Jatinangor Town Square (Jatos), accompanied by Deputy Regent M. Fajar Aldila.

This innovation is the result of collaboration between the Sumedang Regional Government and Nusantara Digital Solusi (NDS), and is designed as a strategic medium for disseminating government information in a broader, faster, and more educational manner.

“This TV Robot serves as a medium for information, education, entertainment, and promotion. The public can find out what we are doing, give suggestions, and even criticism so that we can continue to improve public services,” said Regent Dony in his speech.

He also emphasized the importance of regularly updating content.benefit of the government and the community.

"I ask that officers routinely update the content, especially educational information. This is part of our spirit of delivering open and participatory governance," he said.

TV Robot is not only available at Jatos, but also at several other strategic locations such as Asia Plaza, Sumedang Public Service Mall (MPP), Umar Wirahadikusumah Regional General Hospital, and Sumedang Government Center (PPS) Setda.

NDS Director Darmadi expressed his pride in supporting Sumedang's digitalization efforts towards becoming a Smart City.

“With TV Robot, the public can access information quickly, and the government can receive direct feedback from the public, including through Indochat or visual reports,” he explained.

He also added that in the future, TV Robot will be equipped with interactive features and connected to other digital media such as videotrons that will be built in the Binokasih area.

“We hope Sumedang becomes a national benchmark. If successful, this concept can be replicated by other regions across Indonesia,” he said.

With the presence of TV Robot, the Sumedang Regency Government is not only providing an information tool but also a symbol of openness, innovation, and collaboration between the government and the community.
